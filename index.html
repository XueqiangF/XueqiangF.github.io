Brief Bio

Xue-Qiang Fan is currently a PHD student major in School of Computer and Information, College of Computer Science, Hefei University of Technology. He received B.S. degree in Automation from Henan University of Urban Construction, China, in 2019, and received M.S. degree in Control Science and Engineering from Zhejiang University of Technology, Hangzhou, China, in 2022. Currently, his research interests include Pattern Recognition, Bioinformatics, Deep Learning, Computational Imaging, and Polarization Information Processing.
