ResearchGate  /  Github  / 

Xue-Qiang Fan is a PHD student major in School of Computer and Information, College of Computer Science, Hefei University of Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Zhong-Yi Guo. He received B.S. degree in automation from Henan University of Urban Construction, China, in 2019, and received M.S. degree in control science and engineering from Zhejiang University of Technology, Hangzhou, China, in 2022. His research interests include pattern recognition, bioinformatics, machine learning, and polarization imaging.
